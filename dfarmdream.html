D'Farm Dream

Project Manager and QA Manual

A comprehensive management and quality assurance role for an IoT-based poultry ERP system, transforming a complex, multi-module platform into a reliable, user-friendly experience through systematic project coordination and rigorous testing protocols.

Role: Project Manager & QA Manual Date: October 2024

WHY I MANAGED THIS PROJECT

Throughout my involvement with D'Farm Dream, Indonesia's pioneering modern egg-laying poultry farm utilizing an IoT-based system, I took on the critical role of Project Manager and QA Manual to ensure seamless delivery and optimal system quality. The project required coordinating multiple teams, managing complex timelines, and implementing rigorous testing protocols for a comprehensive agricultural platform integrating employee workflows, coop and warehouse management, as well as financial and investor monitoring.

In my dual role as Project Manager and QA Manual, I focused on streamlining team communication, establishing clear project milestones, and conducting thorough manual testing to identify and resolve system issues before deployment. My goal was to ensure the platform met all functional requirements while maintaining high standards of usability and reliability, allowing investors and farm operators to efficiently track chicken health, feeding, and environmental conditions in real-time.

This project provided valuable experience in managing cross-functional teams while maintaining strict quality control, building a foundation for successful project delivery and ongoing system improvements.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT APPROACH

Team Coordination

Led daily standups, sprint planning, and retrospectives to ensure smooth collaboration between development, design, and testing teams.

Timeline Management

Developed and maintained project schedules, identified critical path dependencies, and managed stakeholder expectations throughout the development cycle.

Risk Mitigation

Proactively identified potential risks, developed contingency plans, and ensured project deliverables met quality standards and deadlines.

Quality Assurance

Established comprehensive testing protocols, conducted manual testing sessions, and maintained detailed bug tracking and resolution processes.

QUALITY ASSURANCE METHODOLOGY

01

Test Planning

Created comprehensive test plans covering functional, integration, and user acceptance testing scenarios for all system modules.

02

Manual Testing

Executed systematic manual testing across web and mobile platforms, focusing on user workflows, data integrity, and system performance.

03

Bug Tracking

Documented and prioritized issues using structured bug reports, coordinated with development team for timely resolution.

04

Validation

Performed regression testing and final validation to ensure all features worked correctly before deployment to production.
